== Description ==

With an EMI calculator that rapidly calculates the monthly payment on their home loan, personal loan, or car loan, you may dazzle website visitors and customers.

Shortcode - **[emi_calculator]**

<h3>Features</h3>

<ul>
<li>Mobile friendly UI</li>
<li>Customize calculator body, form and result background color</li>
<li>Customize input field symbol background color and input field border color</li>
<li>Select chart type</li>
<li>Change Chart principal and intereset amount color</li>
<li>Change slider activ, progress and slider thumb color</li>
<li>Display result with chart</li>
<li>Customize result text setting</li>
<li>Customize result chart text setting</li>
<li>Set min and max loan amount</li>
<li>Set min and max interest rate</li>
<li>Set min and max year and month tenure</li>
<li>EMI Calculator</li>
</ul>
